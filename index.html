How to Play

To play the Piconnera Gacha, you must be a holder of a Foundation auction Stamp or Foundation NFT from Chionnera.
Stamps are airdropped to all participants who place a bid on a Foundation auction. If you do not have a Stamp or Foundation NFT, please wait for the next auction to participate, or purchase an NFT from someone else.

Foundation NFT = 2 Pulls
Auction Stamp = 1 Pull
